iBrand – Real-Time Content Automation Platform

Kafka · Spark · Airflow · MongoDB · FastAPI · Docker

Problem. Brands struggle to keep up with real-time news and social trends while maintaining a consistent tone of voice.

What I did. Co-designed a pipeline that ingests articles from multiple news and social sources, streams the data via Kafka, processes it with Spark, enriches it with NLP signals and stores it in MongoDB for fast querying.

Result. Enabled automated, brand-aligned social content generation on top of clean, queryable article data, reducing manual research time for marketers.

AI-Driven Property Deal Finder

Python · Airflow · AWS · XGBoost · SHAP · FastAPI · Twilio

Problem. Realtors needed a way to spot under-priced properties in a noisy, fast-moving Canadian housing market.

What I did. Built a workflow that aggregates listing data, trains a regression model (XGBoost) to estimate fair price, explains model behaviour with SHAP and sends SMS alerts via Twilio when prices look anomalously low.

Result. Helped identify potential deals by flagging listings priced significantly below the model’s predicted value, giving realtors a faster starting shortlist.
